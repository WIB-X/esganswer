The 1-page ESG data folder that answers every customer questionnaire

India · 2026 · ESG Answer Editorial

The secret that consultants won't tell you: every ESG questionnaire asks for roughly the same 20 data points. Capture them once in a single sheet, and you'll answer any future request in minutes instead of days.

Why a "folder", not a platform

When the second and third questionnaires arrive, suppliers who built a reusable data sheet answer fast; everyone else starts from scratch each time. You don't need software — you need one well-organised spreadsheet you update once a year. That's your evidence folder.

The fields to track (copy this into a spreadsheet)

Environment

  • Annual electricity (kWh) — from 12 months of bills
  • Diesel (litres), petrol (litres), LPG (kg or cylinders), CNG (kg)
  • Water consumed (kL), waste to landfill (tonnes)
  • Your calculated Scope 1 and Scope 2 (tCO₂e)

Social

  • Total employees; number of women; contract types
  • Minimum-wage compliance (Y/N)
  • Policies: workplace safety, anti-harassment (POSH), no child/forced labour (Y/N + date adopted)
  • Safety incidents in the year (number)

Governance

  • Company legal name, GSTIN, sector, sites
  • Code of conduct (Y/N); named ESG/sustainability contact
  • Any fines or convictions (Y/N)
Keep one extra column: "evidence link" — where the proof lives (bill, policy PDF, payroll). When a customer asks for backup, you have it instantly.

The yearly rhythm

  1. Once a year, total up the 12 months of bills and headcount and update the sheet.
  2. Recalculate Scope 1 + Scope 2 (the free tool does this in minutes).
  3. Review the policy rows — turn any "planned" into "done" where you've made progress.

That's the entire system. Twenty minutes a year converts ESG questionnaires from a panic into a routine task.
