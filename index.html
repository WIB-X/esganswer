Common questions

Why did my customer suddenly ask for this?

Large listed companies in India must report ESG data under SEBI's BRSR framework, and from FY2026-27 that extends to their value chain — their significant suppliers. If you export, your buyers face the EU's CSRD with the same effect. You're not being singled out; it's becoming a routine part of being a supplier.

Do I need to buy ESG software?

No. The expensive platforms are built for the large company doing the asking. To answer, you need your real numbers, honest policy answers, and a clean summary — exactly what this free tool gives you.

Why not just use a free UK/US carbon calculator?

India's grid is ~3× more carbon-intensive (0.7117 kg CO₂/kWh per CEA V21, vs ~0.2). A Western tool understates your footprint by ~70% and gives your customer a number they can't use. This tool uses India's official CEA grid emission factor.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. Everything is calculated in your browser. Nothing you type here is sent to a server or saved.
